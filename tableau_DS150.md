Use Tableau to recreate a chart as closely as you can. This exercise is/was part of the final exam for students. They are given 30-40 minutes to complete the exercise.

The data and image you need to recreate are contained in [this Tableau workbook](DS150_Online_Teacher_Challenge.twbx)


## Background

fivethirtyeight.com published an article about America's favorite start wars movies and their characters. We have cleaned and compacted some of the data to be more user friendly. Your job is to use Tableau to recreate a chart from the article.

### Challenge Steps

- [ ] Download the [Tableau workbook](DS150_Online_Teacher_Challenge.twbx).
- [ ] The workbook contains the necessary data and an image of the chart you need to recreate in Tableau to the best of your ability.
- [ ] Spend no more than 60 minutes on the exercise.
- [ ] Submit the Tableau workbook.
